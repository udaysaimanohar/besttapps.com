1. Information We Collect

We collect information so we can provide, improve, personalize and secure the Services. The types of information we collect include:

a) Information you provide directly

  • Account details: name, email address, profile information you optionally provide.
  • Communications: messages and chat logs you send or receive within the apps, customer support conversations.
  • Voice data: audio captured during in-app calls when you use voice features; used for call delivery, quality and safety monitoring.
  • Payment details: payment confirmations, purchase receipts and transaction metadata. (We do not store full card numbers — payment processing is handled by third parties.)

b) Information collected automatically

  • Device & technical data: device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (including Advertising ID), IP address, and network information.
  • Usage data: logs of app features you use, pages visited, clicks, error and crash reports, performance metrics.
  • Analytics & diagnostics: data collected by services such as Firebase and Google Analytics to help us measure and improve the product.

c) Information from third parties

We may receive information from service providers, advertising partners, or fraud detection services (for example Firebase, AdMob, Google Analytics, payment gateways).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core service delivery: to enable chat, audio and video communications, real-time voting, account access and profile features.
  • Safety, moderation & fraud prevention: to detect and prevent abuse, enforce our Terms of Service, investigate suspicious activity and protect users. Chat logs and voice data may be reviewed as part of these processes.
  • Analytics & product improvement: to understand usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop features (via Firebase, Google Analytics).
  • Advertising & personalization: to deliver and measure ads served through AdMob and other partners, and to improve ad relevancy where permitted.
  • Payments & billing: to process purchases, refunds and manage subscriptions in conjunction with third-party payment processors.
  • Communication: to respond to support requests, send transactional messages, updates about changes to the Services, and security alerts.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data in the following circumstances:

a) With service providers

We share information with third-party vendors that perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Cloud storage and hosting providers
  • Firebase (analytics, crash reporting, auth)
  • Google services (AdMob, Google Analytics)
  • Payment processors (payment verification and settlement)
  • Customer support platforms

These providers have access only to the information necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obliged to protect your data.

b) Legal obligations and protection

We will disclose information if required by law, judicial process, or to protect the rights, property or safety of our users or the public.

c) Business transfers

In the event of a merger, sale, acquisition or transfer of assets, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity consistent with this Privacy Policy.
