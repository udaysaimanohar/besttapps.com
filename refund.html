Community Guidelines

Last updated: 2025-11-18

Welcome to Besttapps! These Community Guidelines outline what is expected from everyone using our apps — Bestiee, Pollplay — and our website besttapps.com. Our goal is to maintain a positive, respectful, and safe environment for all users.

1. Be Respectful and Kind

Every user deserves to feel safe and welcomed. You must NOT:

  • Harass, bully, insult, or intimidate others
  • Use abusive, offensive, or discriminatory language
  • Encourage harm, self-harm, or violence

2. No Hate Speech or Discrimination

You must not promote or participate in hate speech based on:

  • Gender, race, religion, ethnicity
  • Caste, disability, nationality
  • Sexual orientation or any protected identity

3. No Sexual Content or Inappropriate Behavior

  • No sexually explicit messages, voice, or images
  • No nudity or pornographic behavior
  • No sexual harassment or solicitation

4. No Illegal or Dangerous Activities

  • Do not promote drugs, scams, or illegal services
  • Do not attempt to buy/sell accounts or virtual currency outside the app
  • No threats, violence, or harmful activities

5. Keep It Real — No Impersonation

  • You may not impersonate another user, celebrity, or official
  • Do not mislead others about your identity or intentions

6. Respect Privacy

  • Never share someone’s personal information without consent
  • Recording calls, chats, or screenshots for harmful use is prohibited

7. No Spam or Misuse of Platform Features

  • No spamming chats with repetitive messages
  • No automated bots, scripts, or manipulation of votes/polls
  • No attempts to hack or reverse engineer the app

8. Safety in Voice & Chat Interactions

Voice chats, audio calls, and messaging are monitored for safety and moderation. Violations may result in immediate suspension.

  • No abusive tone or threatening language
  • No attempts to provoke or insult others
  • No sharing of inappropriate recordings

9. Reporting Violations

If you see something unsafe or inappropriate, please report it immediately.

Email: support@besttapps.com
Response Time: 48–72 hours

10. Consequences for Violations

Depending on severity, actions may include:

  • Warning or temporary suspension
  • Permanent ban of the account
  • Loss of coins or in-app privileges
  • Reporting to authorities (in cases of illegal activity)

11. Appeals

If you believe an action was taken in error, you can appeal by contacting us with proper details:

Email: support@besttapps.com

12. Updates to These Guidelines

We may update these guidelines regularly to ensure safety and compliance. Changes take effect once posted.
