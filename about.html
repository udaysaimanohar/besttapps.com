About Besttapps

We create interactive mobile experiences that bring people together — products designed for engagement, fairness, and delightful user experiences.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Besttapps began with a simple idea: transform passive audiences into active participants. We build lightweight, secure, and fast mobile applications that make live interaction meaningful — from voting and participatory shows to live social conversations. Our first products, Bestiee and Pollplay, reflect this vision.

Mission

To craft delightful digital experiences that enrich everyday life. We combine creative design with modern engineering to build products people enjoy and trust.

Vision

To lead the future of interactive entertainment — where audiences shape outcomes and conversations become opportunities for connection.

What we build

We specialise in:

  • Real-time interaction systems for live shows and broadcasts (polling, voting, live results).
  • Social & communication platforms with audio/video/chat and built-in safety features.
  • Verification and monetization flows for creators and responders.
  • Scalable, privacy-conscious backend systems supporting thousands of concurrent users.
