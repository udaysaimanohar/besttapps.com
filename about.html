About Varshith Fruits & Vegetables Private Limited

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Varshith Fruits and Vegetables Private Limited began as a visionary technology company committed to creating engaging mobile applications that bring people together. From humble beginnings, we have grown into an innovative studio behind Bestiee and Pollplay, with more exciting titles on the horizon.

Mission

Our mission is to craft delightful digital experiences that enrich everyday life. We believe in combining creativity with cutting-edge technology to build apps that users love.

Our Products

Both Bestiee and Pollplay are owned and operated by Varshith Fruits and Vegetables Private Limited. We continuously improve these products and explore new opportunities to expand our portfolio.
